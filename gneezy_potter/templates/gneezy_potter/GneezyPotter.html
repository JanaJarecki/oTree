Next is an investment task (it also counts toward your bonus). You receive 100 pennys. Your task is to decide which part of the 100 pennys (between 0 pennys and 100 pennys) you wish to keep, and which part you wish invest in a lottery. The lottery is the following:

You have a chance of 2/3 (67%) to lose the amount you invested. You have a chance of 1/3 (33%) to win two and a half times (2.5x) the amount you invested. You keep the amount not invested.

What can I earn?

When you decide to invest X pennys (0 ≤ X ≤ 100) in the lottery, your earnings are:

  • With probability 67% the lottery pays zero. Youou earn the amount that you did not invest.
  • With probability 33% the lottery pays 2.5X. You earn +2.5 x X plus the amount that you did not invest.

Please to enter the amount that you decide to invest into the lottery (0 ≤ X ≤ 100) in the form below.
