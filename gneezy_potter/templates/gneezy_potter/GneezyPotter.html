Next is an investment task. The task has 9 rounds. For each round you will receive 100 cents cents. You decide which part of the 100 cents (between 0 cents and 100 cents) you wish to keep and which part you wish invest in a lottery like the following:

You have a chance of 2/3 (67%) to lose the amount you invested and
a chance of 1/3 (33%) to win two and a half times (2.5) the amount you invested.

Suppose you decide to invest X cents (0 ≤ X ≤ 100) in the lottery. You are asked to enter the amount into the form below.

Your earnings are determined as follows. Say you invested an amount of X in the lottery:

  • With probability 67% the lottery pays zero and you earn the amount that was not invested.
  • With probability 33% the lottery pays 2.5X and your earn +2.5X plus the amount that was not invested.
