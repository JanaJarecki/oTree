Next is an investment task, it is relevant for your bonus. You receive 100 pennys. Your task is to decide which part of the 100 pennys (between 0 pennys and 100 pennys) you wish to keep, and which part you wish invest in a lottery like the following:

You have a chance of 2/3 (67%) to lose the amount you invested and
a chance of 1/3 (33%) to win two and a half times (2.5) the amount you invested.

What can I earn?

Suppose you decide to invest X pennys (0 ≤ X ≤ 100) in the lottery. Your earnings are determined as follows.

  • With probability 67% the lottery pays zero, then you earn the amount that you did not invest.
  • With probability 33% the lottery pays 2.5X, then you earn +2.5 x X plus the amount that you did not invest.
